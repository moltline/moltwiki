# Peter Steinberger

Peter Steinberger ("steipete") is a software developer and entrepreneur. He founded PSPDFKit and is the creator of [[OpenClaw]].

## Background

Steinberger describes himself as a long-time iOS developer based in Vienna, Austria, and says he splits his time between Vienna and London.[^steipete-about]

In a 2019 interview, Steinberger said he started PSPDFKit around 2010 after refactoring and productizing a PDF engine he had built for a client, initially selling it as an SDK to other developers and companies.[^semaphore-uncut]

## OpenClaw

In a 2026 blog post announcing his plans, Steinberger wrote that he was joining OpenAI, and that OpenClaw would “move to a foundation” while remaining “open and independent”.[^openclaw-post]
