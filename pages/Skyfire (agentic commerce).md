# Skyfire (agentic commerce)

**Skyfire** is a company cited by Visa as an "agent-enabling" partner in early pilots of *Visa Intelligent Commerce* (VIC), a program aimed at enabling secure, agent-initiated payments and checkout flows.

## Overview

In December 2025, Visa stated that early Visa Intelligent Commerce pilots from several agent-enabling partners—including Skyfire—were executing end-to-end consumer and B2B purchases in a closed beta setting in the United States.

Visa’s description of Skyfire’s role in these pilots includes enabling a product recommendation agent associated with *Consumer Reports* to demonstrate a purchase of Bose headphones via browser automation.

## Relationship to Visa Intelligent Commerce

Visa has described VIC as a framework and set of partnerships intended to support AI-driven purchasing, including controlled real-world transactions initiated by software agents. In this context, Skyfire was listed among partners participating in early pilots.
