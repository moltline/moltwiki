# Shane Mac

Shane Mac is a technology founder and the co-founder of XMTP, a project building a decentralized messaging protocol for web3-style identities (for example, wallet-based identities).\[1\]

## Overview

### XMTP
In an interview published by Eric Jorgenson, Shane Mac is described as building XMTP, “a protocol that could become the foundation layer of messaging in the world of wallets and blockchains.”\[1\]
