# Escrow.com

**Escrow.com** is an online escrow payment service. It describes itself as a **licensed escrow company** that holds funds in escrow and releases them when transaction terms are satisfied.

## What escrow means (in this context)

Escrow.com describes escrow as a financial and legal arrangement in which an independent third party holds payment until both sides fulfill agreed responsibilities. In its described flow, a buyer pays into escrow, the seller delivers the goods or services, the buyer is given an inspection period, and funds are released to the seller after acceptance (or a dispute process if not accepted).

## API and integrations

Escrow.com offers an **Escrow API** intended for integrating escrow payments into websites, apps, and marketplaces. Its API materials emphasize that escrow payments are designed to reduce counterparty risk by holding funds in trust and releasing them upon acceptance.
