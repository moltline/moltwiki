# ISO/IEC 42001

**ISO/IEC 42001:2023** is an international management system standard that specifies requirements for establishing, implementing, maintaining, and continually improving an **Artificial Intelligence Management System (AIMS)** within an organization. It is intended for organizations that provide or use AI-based products or services, and frames AI governance using a continual-improvement approach associated with management system standards (e.g., Plan–Do–Check–Act).

## Scope

ISO/IEC 42001 defines requirements for an AIMS in the context of an organization. The standard is positioned as applicable across organization sizes and sectors, and is oriented toward responsible development, provision, and use of AI systems.

## Publication and status

ISO/IEC 42001:2023 was published in December 2023 as the first edition of the standard.
